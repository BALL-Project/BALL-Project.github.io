Fast Numerical Calculation of Solvent Accessible Surface Area.

These functions are loosely based on the algorithm by Eisenhaber, Lijnzaad, Argos, Sander, and Scharf ("The Double Cubic Lattice Method: Efficient Approaches to numerical Integration of Surface Area and Volume and to Dot Surface Contouring of Molecular Assemblies", J. Comput. Chem. (1995), <b> 15 </b>, 273-284) and Eisenhaber and Argos ("Improved Strategy in Analytic Surface Calculation for Molecular Systems: Handling of Singularities and Computational Efficiency" J. Comput. Chem. (1993), 14 , 1272-1280).
